FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to things people often ask before reaching out.

What does a fractional engagement look like?

We embed directly with your team on a part-time basis. This typically means joining relevant meetings, having a dedicated Slack channel, and working a set number of hours per week or month. We flex up during busy periods and provide steady support during maintenance phases.

How long do engagements typically last?

Most clients work with us for 6-12+ months. RevOps transformations take time to implement properly and show results. That said, we don't lock you into long-term contracts—we earn continued engagement by delivering value.

Do you only work with HubSpot users?

While HubSpot is our deepest expertise, we work across modern go-to-market tech stacks. If you're using Salesforce, Segment, or other tools alongside or instead of HubSpot, we can still help—especially with data architecture, integrations, and operational design.

What size companies do you work with?

We focus on mid-market B2B companies—typically $5M-$100M in revenue. These organizations are complex enough to benefit from sophisticated RevOps but often can't justify or attract full-time senior RevOps talent.
